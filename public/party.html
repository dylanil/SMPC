Cravage Participant ? - demo

Contribute to a Cravage round - a cryptographic average. Your figure never leaves this browser; you submit only a signed, masked share. Two keypairs are generated locally: one for ECDH mask derivation, one for ECDSA signing of every submission. Private halves stay in this tab.

Step 1 - Enter invite code & private figure

Use a test figure only - not real, sensitive, regulated, or production data. Your input stays in your browser, but this demo does not verify whether the value is truthful or reasonable.

Step 2 - Register signing key & receive bearer token

Step 3 - ECDH key exchange & mask derivation

Step 4 - Compute masked share locally

Step 5 - Sign and submit masked share

Step 6 - Independently verify every share's signature and the sum

About this demo

An educational proof-of-concept, not an audited or production service. In a real round, your raw figure stays in your browser and every result is independently verifiable, but the demo has deliberate limits: it assumes honest inputs, needs all participants online at once, and can't stop an impostor who registers before the real participant. Full threat model: README · SECURITY.

Demo provided as-is; do not rely on it for real, confidential, regulated, or production use.

No accounts or analytics. Logs are minimal and ephemeral; IPs are used for rate limiting. Functional auth cookies are used only when password protection is enabled.
